Carbon Emission Audit

Calculate your organization's greenhouse gas emissions using the GHG Protocol Scope 1, 2 & 3 methodology. Get comprehensive insights into your environmental impact.

What you'll measure:

  • Scope 1: Direct emissions from fuel and equipment
  • Scope 2: Indirect emissions from purchased energy
  • Scope 3: Other indirect emissions from supply chain and operations
  • Export results and save audit reports to MongoDB
